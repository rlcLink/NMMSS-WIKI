This function produces a formatted report listing of inventory and
material balances by applying transactions for the current reporting
period to the balances generated for the previous reporting period at a
DOE project specific level.

**Use Case(s)**

- UC5.1 - The Nuclear Material Analyst executes a miscellaneous report.

**System Requirements(s)**

- RPT1 - The NMMSS shall have the capability to generate DOE project specific inventory and material balances (allotment).  Inventory and material balances will be generated by applying transactions for the current reporting period to the balances generated for the previous reporting period (at a DOE project specific level).
