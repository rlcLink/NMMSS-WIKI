This function produces a formatted report of the complete picture of the project status of all material at a facility. This report uses a tails assay of 0.25% U-235 for computation of uranium feed and separate work values.

**Use Case(s)**

- UC4.1 - The Nuclear Material Analyst executes a Transaction Processing report.

**System Requirement(s)**

This report shall show feed, separative work, and dollar values. For each DOE project, inventory quantities and dollar values by inventory category and material type will be displayed. Total inventory in each project will also be listed. A summary showing total material at the facility by generic material type will be provided.
