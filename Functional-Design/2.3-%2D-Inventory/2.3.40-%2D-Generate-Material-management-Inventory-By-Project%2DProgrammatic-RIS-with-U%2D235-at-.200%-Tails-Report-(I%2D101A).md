<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/233/" target="_blank">2.3.40 - Generate Material management Inventory By Project-Programmatic RIS with U-235 at .200% Tails Report (I-101A)</a>-This function produces a formatted report of nuclear material inventory at the project level. This report uses a tails assay of 0.2% U-235 for computation of uranium feed and separative work values.

**Use Case(s)**

<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/689/" target="_blank">UC4.1</a> - The Nuclear Material Analyst executes a Transaction Processing report.

**System Requirement(s)**

<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/1032/" target="_blank">INVPRO16</a> - All inventory reports will indicate if data is reported or generated

<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/738/" target="_blank">INVRPT44  </a> - The NMMSS shall have the ability to produce a materials management inventory by project-programmatic RIS with U-235 at .200%, .250%, or .300% tails report (I-101A, I-101B, and I-101C) that will display nuclear material inventory at the project level. Inventory quantities and values within each RIS will be reports by inventory category and material type. The report will show feed and separative work and dollar values. A summary showing totals for each generic material type will also be included.
