<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/212/" target="_blank">2.3.19 - Generate Composition of Ending Inventory for Licensee Waste Management Accounts (1-02ID)</a> -This function produces a formatted report of the inventories of nuclear materials at waste management sites. The balances are computed from the inventory and transaction information.


**Use Case(s)**

<a href="https://dev.azure.com/Link-Technologies/NMMSS%20Requirements/_workitems/edit/689/" target="_blank">UC4.1</a> - The Nuclear Material Analyst executes a Transaction Processing report.

**System Requirement(s)**

- INVPRO16 - All inventory reports will indicate if data is reported or generated

- INVRPT13 - The NMMSS shall have the ability to produce an inventory summary and consolidated report of composition of ending inventory for contractors, licensees, waste management report (1-021, 1-021G) that will describe the inventories of nuclear material held by facilities. Information contained in this report will be compiled from reported inventory data and contains a consolidated report on each type of nuclear material represented in the inventories of a group of facilities.
